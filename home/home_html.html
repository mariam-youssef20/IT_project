Animals

Animals have different body features that help them survive in their environments.

For example, camels have long eyelashes and wide feet to protect them from sand and heat, while penguins have thick feathers and fat to stay warm in icy places.

Animals act differently based on where they live.

Some animals, like bats, are active at night to stay cool or avoid predators, while others, like bears, hibernate during winter to save energy when food is hard to find.

They also live in different habitats depending on what they need to survive.

Monkeys live in trees where they can find fruits and swing from branches, while dolphins live in oceans and use their fins to swim and catch fish.
